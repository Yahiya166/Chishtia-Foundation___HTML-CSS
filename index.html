Sufi Rang Festival

EMPOWERING ARTISTS

Chishty Foundation began the International Sufi Rang Festival 10 years ago in Dargah Ajmer Sharif, as a celebration and showcase of the many strands and “colours” of creative expression found across time and space from the world of Islamic mysticism. Bringing together ancient and contemporary works, this annual festival aims to preserve and inspire the tradition of sacred art among the youth of today – and tomorrow, while promoting peace, beauty. compassion, service to humanity and Earth stewardship between spiritual traditions and communities...
