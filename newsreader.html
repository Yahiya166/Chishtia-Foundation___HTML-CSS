ACTIVELY PRACTICING GLOBAL SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY - CHERY INTERPRETS GREAT LOVE WITHOUT BORDERS

In September, Chery held a charity performance in cooperation with Maria Ayuda, a Chilean-run child welfare organization. Chery gave away a Tiggo 2 model as a prize for free, calling on people to actively donate money to help children in need.

Event poster

Chery has been deeply engaged in the Chilean market for 14 years. While providing excellent products and services to Chilean consumers, Chery has always been concerned about the development of Social undertakings in Chile. Since this year, in addition to the five-year cooperation with UC team in Chile, Chery has also sponsored a youth football school to let more children experience the fun of football. In addition, Chery donated two units of Tiggo 8 to the Municipal government of San Diego for city security patrols, demonstrating the company's active participation in social development.

This year also marks the 20th anniversary of the implementation of Chery's global strategy. With its rapid development, Chery has carried out various forms of social responsibility practice in many countries around the world, actively seeking the convergence point of corporate development and social development, and striving to become an automobile brand with care and responsibility.

Chery pays great attention to the development of sports. It has successively cooperated with the Philippine Women's Volleyball Super League, Costa Rica's CSC (Club Sport Cartaginés) and Ecuador's IDV (Independiente Del Valle). While supporting these teams to continue to achieve excellent results, it also actively carries out a series of sports public welfare activities.

Chery joins hands with partners to fight COVID-19

In the face of disasters, Chery has always appeared in the front line of support. When the 2012 Chile earthquake, the 2016 Ecuador earthquake and the 2017 Peru flood occurred, Chery immediately provided material and financial assistance to the people in the disaster areas, and actively supported disaster relief and post-disaster reconstruction. In particular, in the global fight against COVID-19 in 2020, Chery sent more than 1 million pieces of medical supplies to more than 20 countries and regions, and helped overseas markets participate in the dual "defense battle" of fighting COVID-19 and resuming work by sharing experience and humanitarian assistance.

Employees of Chery's Russian subsidiary

Meanwhile, Chery has created more than 10,000 jobs outside China. 90% of the employees of Chery's Russian subsidiary are Russian local employees. Chery’s Brazilian subsidiary has created more than 3,000 jobs, providing a large number of local social employment and establishing a good brand image for Chinese brands in overseas markets.

Chery overview

Chery is a Chinese automobile brand with global influence. Chery has established a research and development team of more than 5,500 people through five global research and development bases, and has the most powerful research and development strength of engine, transmission, chassis and other core technologies among Chinese automobile brands. Chery is also the first Chinese automobile company to export vehicles, CKD parts, engines and vehicle manufacturing technology and equipment to the world. Up to now, Chery has exported its vehicles to more than 80 countries and regions, and has set up 10 overseas factories, more than 1,500 overseas dealers and service outlets, with nearly 9.5 million users worldwide, ranking first in the number of passenger cars exported from China for 18 consecutive years.
